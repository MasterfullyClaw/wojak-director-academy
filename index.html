Wojak Director GPT

What Is This?

The Wojak Director is a custom ChatGPT agent built specifically for creating Wojak-style cinematic AI videos. It already knows:

  • The exact Wojak character description (face, body, style)
  • The Quiet Window color grade and cinematic lighting system
  • All camera angles, expressions, and visual rules
  • How to generate image prompts AND video prompts
  • The consistency rules that keep your scenes looking like one video

You don't need to know any of this yourself. The agent handles it. You just answer its questions about YOUR story.

How to Use It

  ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
  │                                                     │
  │   ① Open the Wojak Director GPT link below          │
  │                     ↓                               │
  │   ② It asks: "What is your video about?"            │

Tips for Best Results

💡

Know your story first. The GPT will ask you — have your concept, scene ideas, and wardrobe ready before starting.

🔄

Go scene by scene. Don't rush. Generate one image, check it, then move to the next.

👕

Same wardrobe every scene. Tell the GPT once and it locks it. This is what keeps your character consistent.

📱

Green screen for devices. If a scene has a phone or laptop, tell the GPT — it'll add green screen automatically for you to replace later.

① Prerequisites — Decide These First

Before you touch the prompts, you need to know what you're making. Answer these yourself — the prompt will ask you for them.

💡

Video Concept

One sentence. What is this video about?

"The daily routine of someone who chose discipline over comfort"
🎭

Target Emotion

What should the viewer feel at the end?

Motivated? Reflective? Angry? Inspired? Pick one.
🎬

Scene Count

How many scenes? 6-10 is the sweet spot for a 60-90 second video.

8 scenes = 8 images = 8 video clips
👕

Wardrobe

One outfit description that stays the same in EVERY scene.

"Dark crewneck sweater, tailored dark pants, white sneakers, smartwatch"
📋

Scene Ideas

For each scene, know: one action + one environment + one emotion.

Scene 1: "Waking up, bedroom, peaceful" · Scene 2: "Working at laptop, cafe, determined"
Once you have these, the prompts do the rest. You paste the prompt, the LLM asks you questions, you answer with your prerequisites, it generates your final prompts.
