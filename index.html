① Prerequisites — Decide These First

Before you touch the prompts, you need to know what you're making. Answer these yourself — the prompt will ask you for them.

💡

Video Concept

One sentence. What is this video about?

"The daily routine of someone who chose discipline over comfort"
🎭

Target Emotion

What should the viewer feel at the end?

Motivated? Reflective? Angry? Inspired? Pick one.
🎬

Scene Count

How many scenes? 6-10 is the sweet spot for a 60-90 second video.

8 scenes = 8 images = 8 video clips
👕

Wardrobe

One outfit description that stays the same in EVERY scene.

"Dark crewneck sweater, tailored dark pants, white sneakers, smartwatch"
📋

Scene Ideas

For each scene, know: one action + one environment + one emotion.

Scene 1: "Waking up, bedroom, peaceful" · Scene 2: "Working at laptop, cafe, determined"
Once you have these, the prompts do the rest. You paste the prompt, the LLM asks you questions, you answer with your prerequisites, it generates your final prompts.
